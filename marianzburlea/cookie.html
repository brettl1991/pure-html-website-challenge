What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are used to remember user preferences and to provide website owners with information about how visitors interact with their website.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve your browsing experience on our website. Cookies allow us to remember your preferences, such as your language or font size, and to provide you with personalized content. We also use cookies to track website traffic and to analyze user behavior in order to improve our website and our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use both session cookies and persistent cookies on our website. Session cookies are temporary cookies that are stored on your device until you close your browser. Persistent cookies are cookies that remain on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party cookies on our website, such as those from Google Analytics or social media platforms. These cookies are used to collect information about how visitors use our website, such as which pages they visit and how long they spend on each page. This information is used to improve our website and our services.

How to Manage Cookies

You can control the use of cookies on our website by changing your browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to manage cookies by accepting or rejecting cookies on a case-by-case basis or by rejecting all cookies. However, please note that blocking all cookies may affect your ability to use our website and some features may not function properly.
